About

Hi! I'm Kiet - a designer who cares about every little detail.

BFA in Graphic Design · UX Design minor at the University of Minnesota, College of Design · Class of 2028 · GPA 3.50.

By day I'm a Teaching Assistant at UMN College of Design, a Student Web Coordinator for the Medical School, and a Communications Assistant in the Department of Psychology. By night I'm usually drawing something - or building weird websites like this one.

I care about typography, editorial systems, and digital experiences that feel clearer the longer you look at them. In a recent project I ran usability tests with 10 participants and improved navigation clarity by 25% - the kind of measurable win that makes my heart sing.
